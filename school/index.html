About School

Shri Mohan Ramanna Sonnada Government High School Bhantanur was establshed in 1991.It is loacted in rural area andmanaged by Government of Karnataka. The medium of language is Kannada. The aim of the school is to develop all round personality of the child such as physical, social, emotional, spiritual. The School has tota 10 rooms in that 3 classrooms of classes 8 to 10 and headmaster room and staff room and sports room.The school doesn't have primary section. The School consists of 8 teaching staff and 2 non teaching staff and Head Master. The school have a facilities like a small science lab and a library which having more than 3000 books and ground.The lunch is provided by the Government. There are 144 students in a school of batch 2022-23 of classes 8,9 10.
